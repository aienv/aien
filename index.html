The field of artificial intelligence (AI) has seen tremendous growth and development in recent years.
"The field of artificial intelligence (AI) has seen tremendous growth and development in recent years, thanks to advancements in technology and the availability of massive amounts of data. "

Some of the modern AI tools and their applications.


TensorFlow is an open-source software library for building and deploying machine learning models. Developed by Google, TensorFlow provides a wide range of tools for building and training deep learning models, including a high-level API for building models quickly and easily.

PyTorch is another open-source machine learning framework that has gained popularity in recent years. Developed by Facebook, PyTorch provides a flexible and efficient platform for building and training deep learning models.

H2O is an open-source platform for building and deploying machine learning models. H2O provides a range of tools for data preparation, model building, and model deployment. H2O supports a wide range of algorithms, including deep learning, and allows for distributed computing for high-performance computing environments.
